For over five centuries, Italian brides have offered bomboniere — small silk or tulle packages of ten confetti — to every guest at their wedding. It is a ritual of generosity: the bride and groom share their sweetness with those who have come to witness their union.

The ten almonds cannot be divided. Like the bond of marriage, they are offered whole — a symbol of unity, abundance, and the bittersweet beauty of a life shared.

CAFIERO brings this tradition to your wedding with confetti crafted from the almonds of Avola, Sicily — the finest in the world.
