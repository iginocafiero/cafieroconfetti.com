Citrus Meets Tradition

Limone di Sicilia is the meeting of two Sicilian traditions—the ancient art of confetti-making and the island’s legendary citrus groves. Each almond is infused with the bright essence of sun-ripened lemons, creating a confetto that is both familiar and surprising.

···

Sicilian Citrus & Almonds

The lemons of Sicily are renowned worldwide for their intense fragrance and bright flavor. Combined with the Mandorla di Avola—the prized almond of southeastern Sicily—they create a confetto that captures the island’s essence.

···

The Five Blessings

In Italian tradition, five confetti are given at a celebration—never more, never fewer. Each almond represents a wish for good fortune, a blessing carried from the table into the life ahead.
