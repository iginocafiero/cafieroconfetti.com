Oro Verde di Bronte

On the slopes of Mount Etna, the pistachio trees of Bronte produce what Sicilians call “green gold.” This rare, intensely flavored nut is ground into a fine paste and infused into the sugar coating of each confetto, creating a taste unlike anything else.

···

Mandorla di Avola

Every almond begins in the groves of southeastern Sicily, where the volcanic soil and Mediterranean sun produce the Mandorla di Avola — prized by confectioners for its symmetrical shape, rich flavor, and satisfying crunch.

In Italian tradition, five confetti are given at a celebration — never more, never fewer. The Pistacchio di Bronte unites two of Sicily’s greatest ingredients in a single gesture of generosity.
