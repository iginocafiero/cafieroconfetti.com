The Sicilian Bomboniera

The Ivory Box is the quintessential Italian wedding favor—the bomboniera. Each box holds five confetti di Avola, representing the five blessings wished upon the couple. It is a tradition that stretches back centuries in Sicily, where no wedding is complete without this gesture of sweetness.
